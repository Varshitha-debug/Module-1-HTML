This page provides all the assistance you need to use our portal effectively.

How to Register

  • Go to the "Contact" section.
  • Fill out your name, email, and contact number.
  • Select your event type and choose a date.
  • Submit the form and wait for confirmation.

Features You Can Use

  1. Interactive Gallery: Double-click on any image to enlarge it.
  2. Live Fee Updates: Event fee appears when you select an event.
  3. Geolocation: Click "Find Nearby Events" to get your location.
  4. Storage: Your event choice is saved and auto-filled next time.
